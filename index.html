About Us

Maheshmati Tribal Farmer's Producer Company Limited

We are a robust group of 1085 smallholder farmers from the backward district of Mandla in Madhya Pradesh called “Maheshmati Tribal Farmer Producer Company Private Limited”. The 42 villages from where we belong are devoid of access to services for our farming community. The main objective of our company is to function as “one window service provider” for all the crop inputs such as seeds, crop protection measures, farm implements, advisory services for farmers. We have come forward to enable ourselves a better access to markets through collectivisation and promote a culture of entrepreneurship.

WHAT WE DO

Input Supply

We buy essential inputs such as seeds, fertilizer, pesticide in bulk and sell through our retail outlet. The inputs are sold to the members at a price which is far below the market price and thereby help the member farmers to reduce the cost of agri input

Seed Production

Seeds are an essential input for crop production. Farmers’ access to affordable, quality seeds of superior varieties is key towards enhancing agricultural productivity. Our objective is to decentralise seed production

Rice/Millets Processing Unit

The rice/millets processing unit not only helps in transforming the lives of local farmers by providing quality grain and enhancing the economic value of the raw material but also employment to the farming community in the area.
